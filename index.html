Welcome to the Steric Factor

I created this blog without any particular purpose in mind for it, so expect to see poetry, short stories, journal entries, and opinion pieces, all under the same roof. At some future date this page will probably become a portfolio for projects, and the blog will be moved to a subsection of that portfolio. But for now at least, the blog is center stage. I will be toying around with the design of the blog, mostly to see what I am capable of — I will ultimately aim for simplicity in the design.

The blog name is a bad play on words likely to be changed. "Steric" is one of the few words I am aware of in the English language that has a feminine rhyme with my first name. And I like Chemistry, so it was a natural choice.
